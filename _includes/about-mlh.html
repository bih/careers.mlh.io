Major League Hacking (MLH) is the official student hackathon league. Hackathons are weekend-long invention competitions where high school and college students form teams to build next generation apps, robots, websites, and more. More than 65,000 students participate in MLH supported events in North America and Europe each year.

MLH is a certified B Corp with a mission to empower hackers. Our first party events are life-changing experiences for students. We empower them to learn, build, and share the technology of tomorrow with their peers. Our events provide a self-driven environment where students interact with each other outside the confines of traditional academia.
